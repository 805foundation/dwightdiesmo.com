DWIGHT DIESMO BUILDS BUSINESSES

Dwight Diesmo is the active CEO of 805 Foundation, a non-profit conglomerate and full service media agency servicing the latest brands and businesses across the company's 3 locations, and the chairman of Project 619, a student driven organization pursuing student growth through practical experiences, contributing to the community through service and is partnered with other non-profit organiztions such as HOPE.

In the early 2000s, after identifying software as the future of society, Dwight set his sights on providing the Philippines with the education and experience they would need to answer the call of first world countries for software developpers and engineers. He began partnering with universties and hosting Hack Iloilo to set up the Philippines Coding Academy.
